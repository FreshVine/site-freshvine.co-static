End of an Era

Dec 18, 2023

—

by

Paul Prins - Founder
in History

It has been 15 years since we began serving our first customer in March 2nd, 2008. It started with a desire to support the leadership of churches and nonprofits. To help them better care for, and understand, their communities. Over the years that followed, Fresh Vine developed into truly world-class software.

As of today, we have helped hundreds of organizations, and thousands of users manage over…

  • 130,000 individual profiles
  • 12,000 email campaigns with millions of recipients
  • 215,000 events
  • $131,000,000 in donations

As 2023 comes to a close, it is time to announce the end of our membership software. As a business, we do not see a sustainable path forward in 2024. All of our current customers have been personally contacted by me, Paul Prins. I have thanked them for allowing us to support their work all these years. Many have been with us for over a decade. They have also been given information on what this transition process will look like.

Their access to Fresh Vine will continue until March 30, 2024. Our company will cease to exist at the end of 2023. We – okay, it’s Paul – are committed to giving all our current clients 3 free months of service to finalize their 2023 donation statements, and giving them time after the Christmas holiday to find new solutions.

Here is what happens next:

  • All subscription fees will be waived in 2024.
  • After March 30, 2024, the membership service will shut down, and all our copies of your data will be destroyed.
  • Prior to the service shutting down, you can use the built-in export features to archive or migrate your data to another service/s.

From the bottom of my heart, thank you. Supporting the work of your churches, communities, and organizations has been an honor and privilege.
